---
title: How to Cancel A Service
---
1. Login in to your client account on the [Client Portal](https://fourseasonshosting.com/client/clientarea.php)

2. Click on "Services" > "My Services" at top bar on Navigation Bar

3. You'll see the list of services, click on Service that you want to cancel.

<Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-126413f3-60b3-418a-a6a3-c91731a9859b/doc-5ddb8241-3292-4172-aaf3-e39fd0e3f428/1777425238197-htdsojvh2h-pasted-image-1777425236584.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="816" height="601" alt="" caption="This is what it should look like when in the right area!" />

1. Click on Request Cancellation and follow the prompts on the screen

<Callout kind="alert" collapsed="false">
  **Immediate&#x20;**&#x6D;eans it will Terminated at the **next&#x20;**&#x38;pm EST
</Callout>

<Callout kind="danger" collapsed="false">
  When a service is canceled ALL SERVER DATA WILL BE DELETED AND ***UNRECOVERABLE***
</Callout>
