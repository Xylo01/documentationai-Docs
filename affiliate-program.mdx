Learn about how the Affiliate Program works

Affiliate Program is where users can refer new clients to Four Seasons Hosting and get paid 6% of the total, per month as long as the service remain active.

To get started, login or create an Account on the <a href="https://fourseasonshosting.com/client/clientarea.php" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Client Portal</a>. On the Top Navigation Bar you'll see Affiliates.

## Request Withdrawal

Users can request withdrawal at a minimum $25 to their PayPal or Cash App

If less than $25 you can open a ticket, and we can apply it has a credit to your account to use on a new service or invoice.
