In order to connect a system that SSH (Like a Linux server e.g.) you'll need a Terminal service program. We prefer <a href="https://mobaxterm.mobatek.net/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MobaXterm</a> it's simple and most of the guides on Four Seasons Hosting will use this program. Let's connect to our server! Once your server (Linux VPS) has been created and delivered, you'll get the following information Username, password, IP address, all this information will be needed. If you not have seen the email please open a ticket with support team and they will be able to help out.
