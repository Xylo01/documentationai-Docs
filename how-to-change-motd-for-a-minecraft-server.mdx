---
title: change MOTD for a Minecraft server
---
1. Access your server from the <a href="https://gamepanel.fourseasonshosting.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">gamepanel</a>

1) From the left side of the panel, open the **Config Editor** tab and select Server Properties.

1. Enter your desired text in the `MOTD` property and press save.

<Image src="https://docs.fourseasonshosting.com/docs/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F117306917-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FNMJyT1j9vuQnp7boSnoR%252Fuploads%252FvnPB7sWk2KWQEFlG63t3%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D853545f1-d7e3-43d5-a2a1-f73fee442dd3&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=90d4ee24&sv=2" width="2522" height="940" alt="" />



1. Restart your server.

---

You may use a site like <a href="https://mctools.org/motd-creator" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MCTools</a> to customize the text with different colors and styles.
