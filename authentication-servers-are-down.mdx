---
title: Authentication servers are down
---
Here are a few steps you can try to resolve the issue:

1. **Check Your Internet Connection** Restart your router to see if it resolves the problem. Ensure your connection is stable.

2. **Use or Disable a VPN** Try connecting through a VPN, or if you're already using one, disable it. Changing your IP address can help bypass restrictions that might be causing the error.

3. **Check Mojang's Status Updates** Visit the <a href="https://twitter.com/MojangStatus" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Mojang Status Twitter account</a> for updates. If there's downtime, they may have already posted about it.

4. **Try Again Later** If the servers are down, you’ll need to wait until they’re back online and try logging in later.

5. **Contact Minecraft Support** If none of the above methods work and there’s no reported downtime, reach out to <a href="https://help.minecraft.net" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Minecraft Support</a> for further assistance.
