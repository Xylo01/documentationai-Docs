---
title: How to Create an AuthKey for BeamMP
---
Here is how you create an AuthKey for BeamMP and add it to your server. This is required for your server to display in the server browser.

> **Note** Due to recent BeamMP changes, your server version MUST be at least v3.9.0 to be publicly listed in the server browser.

### Creating an AuthKey

1. Go <a href="https://auth.beammp.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a> and log in with your Discord account.

2. Navigate to the Keys page in the sidebar, then click the + icon.

3. Type in your server name, click OK, and then copy your key.

4. Visit the <a href="https://gamepanel.fourseasonshosting.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">**FSH Game Panel**</a> at and select your BeamMP server.

5. In the sidebar, go to Server Options.

6. Enter your key under AuthKey.

7. Click Console in the sidebar, then restart your server.
