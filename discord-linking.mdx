---
title: Discord Linking
---
Linking your client account and discord allows you to open a discord tickets and get the client roles on Discord and allows the system to DM you with information about invoices, due dates, orders and much more.&#x20;

This will also allow you to sign in via discord on the website.

1. To link the account, please log in to the client portal

2. On the Left side menu look for Link Social Media then Discord and to the right of the Link

   <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-126413f3-60b3-418a-a6a3-c91731a9859b/doc-5ddb8241-3292-4172-aaf3-e39fd0e3f428/1777417798115-2rce1vj2iwy-pasted-image-1777417796844.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="337" height="211" alt="" />

3. Once you’ve clicked link, the server will bring up a discord webpage asking you to authorize your account. Click authorize and once done you'll get a notice saying you've linked it Successfully
